Ramos is a Club Legend.Captaining Madrid for 10 years running, he's a formidable force on and off the pitch, striking fear into attackers and defenders alike. his versatility on the wing and ability to make creative plays possible is his strength. He can pick out a pass from 60 yards out- to a T -all while scanning the field around him for space. He usually opts for quick, fast play on the wing to exploit momentary free pace created and hence advance deeper into the attacking third. That, coupled with defence-splitting dribbling prowess, a sledgehammer of a left foot, and an uncanny ability to mark and consequently hack down opposition wingers makes him the king of the position.
